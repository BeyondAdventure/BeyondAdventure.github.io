Need Funding

NASA is divided into several major programs, each of which receives its own funding. Overall NASA funding varies from year to year, but human spaceflight activities receive 50% of the budget. Another 30% goes to robotic missions and scientific research. The remainder is divided among aeronautics, technology development programs, employee salaries, facility management, and other overhead.
