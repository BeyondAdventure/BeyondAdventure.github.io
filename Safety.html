Staying Safe

It's very important that everyone working on a mission is kept safe. so as to ensure that many countermeasures have been put in place. One example is the extremely useful space suits, which keep users safe inside while exploring the space atmosphere. In addition, many scientists established a system known as "Space Lifeguards" to monitor space weather conditions for astronauts.

Keeping The Body Safe

It's time to come back to earth, but your body won't be the same after such an amazing journey. Astronauts will need to go through a recovery process to help their bones and muscles readjust to Earth. The bones will need help getting used to the earth's gravity again. Due to the fact that, while in space, it didn't need to devote such energy to keep you standing. Your muscles will need to regain their strength as they tend to lose muscle mass. Also due to the lack of gravity.
